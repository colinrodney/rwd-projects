Allow Me to Introduce Myself...

My name is Colin Rodney (i'm sure you knew that by now..sheesh). I'm currently employed by one of the largest communications companies in the United States as a Customer Support Specialist. I'm tasked with troubleshooting, testing and resolving issues related to our clients mobile network services and/or their mobile devices.

Fueled by a passion for understanding the "how" and "why" of a problem, my self-taught programming journey began in 2018 when I enrolled in a free course offered by my local public library. From there I began consistently working code challenges on various websites and enrolled in self-paced online courses to guide my learning, reinforce learned concepts, and develop my problem solving and coding skills.

I'm available for freelance work and am actively seeking an opportunity to use my knowledge of Web Design/ User Interface Design alongside my creative process and problem solving skills to move your project from idea to reality.

As a person who is driven by a thirst for knowledge and the determination to succeed, I plan to continue sharpening and refining my existing skills in addition to develping new skills that will allow me to one day compete for senior-level UI role.

Reach out to me at c.renauld.rodney@gmail.com or drop me a line using the contact form located on the contact page. Lets connect!
